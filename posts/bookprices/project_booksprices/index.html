Introduction

I wanted to get informations about books prices, which I’d like to buy in the future. Additionally, it would be nice to see the price curve on the chart.

Functions:

  1. Downloading informations about books (title, price, date) from the acount on the website: ebooki.swiatczytnikow.pl.
  2. Saving data to the database.
  3. Displaying charts - how the price has changed over time for a specific title.
