World Happiness project - about project

The data contain informations which might have influence for people’s happiness score in the selected country. We have 5 reports divided by years, from 2015 to 2019. Acording to data, the happiness score can be estimated from six factors - economic production, social support, life expectancy, freedom, absence of corruption, and generosity. These factors contribute to making life evaluations higher in each country than they are in Dystopia, a hypothetical country that has values equal to the world’s lowest national averages for each of the six factors.
